    Our Story

    Hairy Antler is more than just a distillery—it’s a celebration of Kentucky’s untamed spirit and the craftsmanship that defines it. Nestled among the rolling hills, we craft each of our spirits with the same dedication and attention to detail that our ancestors did, yet with a modern twist that reflects the boldness of our time. Our story began with a desire to honor the traditions of Kentucky’s distilling heritage while pushing the boundaries of what a spirit can be.

    Like the land itself, our distilling process is both wild and refined. We take inspiration from the rich, untamed beauty of Kentucky, drawing from the rugged terrain, the strong winds, and the timeless spirit of those who shaped our history. From the first barrel to the last bottle, every aspect of Hairy Antler is a testament to the enduring legacy of craftsmanship.

    Join us as we continue our journey—untamed yet refined, just like the spirits we craft.
